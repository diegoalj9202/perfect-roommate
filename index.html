The Perfect Roommate

Explore the candidate profile cards. Then compare them and argue who is the best roommate for you (or for a situation your teacher gives you).

Teacher tip: Think about the things you consider are on favor of the candidate you choose. You must be ready to convince others using reasons and examples.
Useful phrases: In my opinion… / I prefer… / That could be a problem because… / On the other hand…
